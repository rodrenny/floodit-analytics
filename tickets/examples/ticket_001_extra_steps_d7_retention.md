# Did the extra-steps starting grant change move D7 retention?

**From:** Marta (product, progression & economy)
**Priority:** high — board review is in two weeks

Hey! Context for those who missed the standup: at some point over the
summer we shipped different *starting grants* of extra steps to new
players — some accounts start with more free steps than others (it's the
`initial_extra_steps` thing the SDK reports).

The theory was that a more generous grant makes the first days less
frustrating, so more people stick around. What I need to know:

> **Do players with a bigger starting grant retain better at day 7?**

Ideally I can open one table and see, per grant bucket: how many new
players got it, and their D1 / D7 retention. If D30 is easy to add, take
it, but D7 is what the board asked about.

One thing I honestly don't know: whether the grant amounts cluster into a
few clean buckets or are all over the place — you'll see it in the data
before I do. Group them however is honest, just don't hide small buckets
that would make the comparison misleading.
