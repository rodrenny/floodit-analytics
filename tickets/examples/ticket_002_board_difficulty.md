# Is the Large board too punishing in quickplay?

**From:** Deniz (game design)
**Priority:** normal

Support keeps forwarding reviews that say the big board is "impossible".
Before I touch the difficulty curve I want numbers, not vibes.

For quickplay, per board size (S/M/L), week over week:

- completion rate (completes / starts)
- how often people retry or reset instead of finishing
- whether players who start on L come back to it or migrate to S/M

If the last one is a pain, skip it — the first two are what I'll act on.
I'd like this as a table I can trend weekly, not a one-off number.
